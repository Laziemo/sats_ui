Multi-Factor Authentication

In order to maintain the integrity of your private key, we only generate it once. If you are unsure of how 2FA works, read the notes before generating a key.





Multi-factor authentication uses a physical 'access token' (phone) to ensure the identify of a user. This security model assumes that only you (the user) have access to your phone.

The QR Code and private key shown above represents a secret associated with your sats.cc account. By scanning this private key on an app like Authy or Google Authenticator, you create a token generator on your phone which is linked to your sats.cc account. A fresh token is generated every 30 seconds which is used to authenticate your physical presence while executing functions on the platform.

This ensures that even if your login credentials are compromised, no critical functions on the platform can be used without verification that it is you, via a physical access token, in this case - your phone.

Ensure that you write down the private key for backup purposes. If you accidently erase your phone, the same key can be used to recover your account authentication.

It is bad practice to store copies of your key online or on devices that regularly access the internet without a particular set of security precautions in place. Secure communication works best when integrity is maintained from all ends.


If you lose your private key OR your phone, its still all good! Just email us at support@sats.cc. As a security measure we will immediately suspend your account from making payments. New key generation will require additional verification and can take upto 0-48 hours.
